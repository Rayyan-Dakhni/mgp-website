Paper Packaging

Our paper packaging solutions are expertly crafted to address the varied needs of retail products, blending sustainability with superior functionality. Utilizing high-quality, eco-friendly materials, our packaging is perfect for an array of products such as groceries, clothing, electronics, and cosmetics. Designed to not only enhance the visual appeal of your products, our packaging also ensures robust protection and durability during transit. In line with our commitment to environmental responsibility, all our paper packaging is recyclable and biodegradable, offering an ideal choice for businesses that prioritize sustainability. Opt for our paper packaging solutions to elevate your brand’s presentation while contributing to a healthier planet.

We leverage state-of-the-art, high-speed presses to deliver premium quality multicolored printing, ensuring your packaging is both visually striking and precise. Our advanced die-cutting and pasting machines further enhance our capabilities, allowing us to meet diverse customer requirements with quick turnaround times. With a rigorous quality control process in place, Multi Garment & Print stands as an excellent choice for all your printing and packaging needs, ensuring that your products make a lasting impression.

Flexographic Printing

Efficiency and cost-effectiveness are at the core of our flexographic printing services. Designed to meet high consumer expectations, our high-speed flexographic printing capabilities allow us to produce large volumes of prints in up to four distinct colors on products of varying shapes and sizes. This method is ideal for producing high-quality, consistent results that meet the demands of modern packaging, offering a reliable solution for businesses seeking both quality and affordability in their printing needs.

UV Coating

UV coating services provide an extra layer of protection and shine to your printed materials, making them more durable and visually appealing. UV coating is a quick-drying, high-gloss finish that not only enhances the vibrancy of colors but also adds a protective shield against scratches, smudges, and other potential damage. Ideal for packaging, labels, and promotional materials, this coating method ensures that your products maintain their premium look and feel, even with frequent handling. By choosing UV coating, you can elevate the overall quality of your printed items, ensuring they stand out on the shelf and leave a lasting impression on your customers.

BOPP Lamination

BOPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene) lamination services provide a sophisticated and durable finish for your packaging materials. This lamination technique enhances the strength and aesthetic appeal of your products, offering a smooth, glossy, or matte surface that is resistant to moisture, oils, and external wear. BOPP lamination is particularly effective for products that require both visual appeal and long-lasting protection, such as food packaging, labels, and retail boxes. By opting for BOPP lamination, you can ensure that your packaging not only looks great but also performs exceptionally well, providing an added layer of quality that reflects the premium nature of your brand.

Die Cutting

Our die cutting services offer precision and creativity in shaping your packaging materials to perfectly match your product’s design and branding needs. Utilizing traditional die cutting technology, we can produce intricate and custom shapes, ensuring that your packaging stands out with unique contours and details. Whether you need custom boxes, labels, or specialty items, our die cutting capabilities allow us to craft packaging solutions that are both functional and visually striking. This process not only enhances the aesthetic appeal of your products but also adds a level of customization that sets your brand apart in the marketplace. With our die cutting services, you can achieve packaging that is as distinctive as your brand.
