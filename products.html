Polyester Strapping

Polyethylene terephthalate, or PET strapping, is a strong and long-lasting packing material used to support and secure cargo during storage and transit. PET strapping is a perfect substitute for steel strapping because of its superior elongation and high tensile strength, which provide increased safety and convenience of usage. It is frequently used for product bundling, palletizing, and packaging reinforcement in a variety of sectors, including manufacturing, shipping, and construction. PET strapping is recyclable and environmentally friendly, ensuring dependable performance and supporting eco-friendly packaging techniques.

We provide high-quality, durable PET strapping material to our clients that enables them to consume less storage space and substantially reduce manual labor and transportation costs as compared to steel strap.
